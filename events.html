Events

Our school hosts a variety of events throughout the year designed to enrich student life, foster community engagement, and celebrate academic and extracurricular achievements. From sports meets and cultural festivals to science fairs and guest lectures, there’s always something exciting happening here.

  1. Home
  2. Events
15 JUN

Annual Science Fair Exhibition

09:00 AM - 04:00 PM

Main Campus Auditorium

The Annual Science Fair Exhibition showcases the innovative projects and experiments created by our students. It’s a platform for young minds to explore scientific concepts, demonstrate creativity, and engage with peers, teachers, and experts. This event inspires curiosity and encourages critical thinking, celebrating the spirit of discovery and learning.

Learn More
22 JUN

Parent-Teacher Conference

01:00 PM - 06:00 PM

Multiple Classrooms

The Parent Teacher Conference provides an important opportunity for parents and teachers to discuss students’ progress, strengths, and areas for improvement. This collaborative meeting fosters a supportive learning environment and strengthens the partnership between home and school to help students achieve their best.

Learn More
30 JUN

Summer Sports Tournament Final

02:30 PM - 05:30 PM

Sports Complex

The Summer Sports Tournament Final marks the thrilling conclusion of the season’s competitions, where the best teams and athletes compete for the championship titles. This exciting event celebrates sportsmanship, teamwork, and athletic excellence, bringing the school community together for a day full of energy and fun.

Learn More
05 JUL

Graduation Ceremony Class of 2025

10:00 AM - 01:00 PM

Central Auditorium

The Graduation Ceremony for Class 2025 is a momentous occasion celebrating the achievements and hard work of our graduating students. Family, friends, and faculty come together to honor their journey and mark the beginning of their next chapter with pride and joy.
