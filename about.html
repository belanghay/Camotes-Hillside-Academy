Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Founded to serve the educational needs of the community, Camotes Hillside Academy has grown over the years into a respected institution known for its commitment to quality education, strong values, and student development. It continues to evolve, embracing new challenges and opportunities to better prepare its students for the future.

1965

Established in 1965, Camotes Hillside Academy has been dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing students' growth for over five decades. From its humble beginnings, it has grown into a respected institution committed to academic excellence and community development.

1982

Founded in 1965, Camotes Hillside Academy began its journey as a small learning institution. By 1982, it expanded its facilities and programs, establishing itself as a leading school committed to quality education and community growth.
